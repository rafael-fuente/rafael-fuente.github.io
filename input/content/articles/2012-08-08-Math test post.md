## Difraction Integral
----


$$
  E{(\mathbf{r},t)} = C \int\nolimits_{S'}^{}  \left(\frac{1}{k \left|{r - r'}\right|} - i\right)   \frac{e^{i k \left|{r - r'}\right|}}{\left|{r - r'}\right|} cos{(\mathbf{r - r'}, \hat{z})}\,  d^{2}r',
\text{   con   }  \hspace{2mm}
  C =  \frac{k E_{0} e^{- i t w}}{2 \pi i}
$$
